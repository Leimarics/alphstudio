Our story

"What began as a party game became a patented innovation — and a way to see yourself in art."

Alphavatar began in Canada when a family noticed that the letters in a name could form the features of a face. What started as a party game became an obsession — and eventually, a patent granted in both Canada and the United States.

Today, every Alph Studio product begins with a name. Every portrait is completely unique — generated by our patented algorithm, refined by human designers.
